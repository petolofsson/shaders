### R88 Kalman Q — numerical stability

Q is computed via `lerp(Q_MIN, Q_MAX, smoothstep(..., P_prev))` — the lerp with a [0,1]
smoothstep argument implicitly clamps Q to [Q_MIN, Q_MAX] = [0.0001, 0.10]. No separate clamp
is required. The recursion `P_new = (1-K)*P_pred` with K ∈ (0,1) (guaranteed since KALMAN\_R
> 0) is monotonically decreasing toward a stable fixed point. No runaway Q observed.

### R61 — Per-pixel Hunt locality (grade.fx CHROMA block)

**STATUS: NOT IMPLEMENTED — REGRESSION AGAINST HANDOFF.**

The HANDOFF documents R61 as shipped with:
- `hunt_la = max(lerp(zone_log_key, lab.x, HUNT_LOCALITY), 0.001)`
- `HUNT_LOCALITY 0.35` knob in creative\_values.fx
- `hunt_la` replacing `zone_log_key` in both `_k` and the fl formula

**Findings in current code:**

1. `hunt_la` — **absent** from grade.fx CHROMA block.
2. `HUNT_LOCALITY` — **not defined** in arc\_raiders/shaders/creative\_values.fx (file has 137
   lines; all knobs verified; HUNT_LOCALITY is not among them).
3. The `_k` / `fl` Hunt formula — **absent**. The HK section (grade.fx:454–459) contains only
   the Hellwig 2022 Helmholtz–Kohlrausch approximation using a fixed `pow(final_C, 0.587)`
   factor and hardcoded `smoothstep(0.0, 0.35, lab.x)`. No per-pixel luminance adaptation.

The HANDOFF note "GZW: SHADOW\_LIFT\_STRENGTH + HUNT\_LOCALITY added (were missing — compile
error)" implies `HUNT_LOCALITY` is referenced somewhere in grade.fx. But it is not present in
the current grade.fx, meaning either (a) the reference was removed and the knob definition was
also never committed, or (b) the HANDOFF entry is ahead of implementation. Either way, R61 is
a paper entry only.

**FAIL — requires investigation and implementation.**
