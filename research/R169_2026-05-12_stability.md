## Task A — Register Pressure (ColorTransformPS)

**Method:** Static source analysis. No RADV shader dump available in this session. Prior hardware baseline: 59 VGPRs / 87 SGPRs, `scratch_en: false` (R116, 2026-05-06). Spill threshold: 128 scalar (SGPR) registers.

**Additions to ColorTransformPS scope since R116 baseline:**
- R125/R126 B-B: two new scalars `sh2_h`, `ch3_h` (grade.fx:523–524)
- R133 Munsell rolloff: one scalar `r133_roll` (grade.fx:510)
- R160 adaptive print stock: two scalars `ps_shoulder`, `ps_cubic` in `ApplyPrintStock` (grade.fx:218–219)
- R162 specular contrast: one scalar `ctx.specular_contrast` in `BuildSceneCtx` (grade.fx:361), one scalar `specular_att` in `ApplyTonal` (grade.fx:447)
- R168 halation restructure: six new scalars (`ring_tight`, `ring_broad`, `tight_luma`, `lore_g`, `lore_b`, `col_tight`, `col_broad`) replacing a simpler structure in `ApplyHalation` (grade.fx:239–248)
- R174 grain: two additional pcg3d hash calls and `silver_boost` scalar (grade.fx:811)

Total new named scalars: ~16–18 above R116 baseline. Compiler register reuse across non-overlapping live ranges keeps the effective count well below the 87+18 = ~105 SGPR estimate. No large arrays, no new global state, no unroll-expansion that would create wide spills.

**Result: PROVISIONAL PASS.** Source-level analysis finds no spill-inducing additions. Hardware dump required for confirmation; estimate remains below 128 SGPR threshold.
